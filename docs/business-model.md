# Business Model: Community Mission Operations Support

## Classification
- Repository: `cloud-itonami-9900`
- ISIC Rev.5: `9900` — activities of extraterritorial organizations and bodies
- Social impact: diplomatic access, aid delivery, transparency

## Customer
- delegations and missions needing operations support
- aid and relief bodies running field operations
- observer and standards missions
- programs that cannot accept closed operations platforms

## Offer
- credential and scope-of-authority management
- robotics-assisted aid-delivery and observation missions
- dispatch and movement records
- public and internal reporting
- role-based access and immutable audit ledger

## Revenue
- self-host setup fee
- managed hosting subscription per mission
- support retainer with SLA
- field-operations integration

## Trust Controls
- a robot action the governor refuses is never dispatched to hardware
- safety-critical actions (cross-border, near civilians, sensitive cargo) require human sign-off
- credentials cannot be issued outside their declared scope
- public reports require source evidence
- sensitive dispatch and personnel data stays outside Git
